Popular Songs

First Class

First Class is a song by American rapper Jack Harlow, released through Atlantic Records and Generation Now as the second single from Harlow's second album Come Home the Kids Miss You on April 8, 2022. The song was produced by Rogét Chahayed, BabeTruth, Charlie Handsome, Jasper Harris and Nickie Jon Pabón, and samples the 2006 Fergie song "Glamorous", which features Ludacris and was produced by Polow da Don. The song went viral on TikTok before its release.

As it Was

As It Was entered at the top of the UK Singles Chart, becoming Styles's second number-one single on the chart after "Sign of the Times" in April 2017 and it has so far spent eight weeks at the top of the UK Singles Chart, becoming the longest-running number-one single of 2022 in the UK. Also, it charted at the top of the Billboard Hot 100, becoming his second number-one single on the chart following "Watermelon Sugar" in August 2020 and it has so far spent three non-consecutive weeks at the top of the Billboard Hot 100

N95

"N95" is a song by American rapper Kendrick Lamar. It was sent to Italian radio airplay through PGLang, Top Dawg Entertainment, Aftermath Entertainment, and Interscope Records as the lead single from his fifth studio album, Mr. Morale & the Big Steppers, on May 20, 2022. The song was produced by Boi-1da, Sounwave, and Jahaan Sweet, while it was additionally produced by Lamar's cousin, fellow American rapper and record producer Baby Keem.
